📋 Terms & Conditions

Effective Date: January 1, 2025 | Last Updated: June 1, 2025

Welcome to Megha Tex. These Terms and Conditions govern the use of our bobbin holder servicing and maintenance services. By engaging Megha Tex for any service, you agree to be bound by these terms. Please read them carefully before submitting your bobbin holders for service.

These terms apply to all service engagements between Megha Tex and textile spinning mills or any other clients seeking bobbin holder servicing in Coimbatore and surrounding areas.

⚙️ Service Terms

1.1 Scope of Service

Megha Tex provides bobbin holder inspection, cleaning, repair, restoration, maintenance, and technical support services. The exact scope of service will be agreed upon before work commences based on the condition of the submitted holders.

  • Services are provided for bobbin holders used in textile spinning mills
  • Each service batch is assessed individually before work begins
  • A service report outlining findings will be provided for each batch
  • Additional work discovered during servicing will be communicated before proceeding
  • Megha Tex reserves the right to decline service on holders beyond economical repair

1.2 Service Confirmation

Service commencement is subject to written or verbal confirmation from the client. Submission of holders to Megha Tex constitutes acceptance of these terms.

1.3 Service Timeline

Estimated service completion timelines are provided at time of order and are subject to the volume and condition of holders received. Emergency service is available at an additional charge.

Megha Tex will not be held liable for production delays caused by service timelines communicated in good faith. Clients should plan for buffer time when scheduling service.

🚚 Delivery Terms

2.1 Collection & Drop-Off

Clients are responsible for safely transporting bobbin holders to our service center. Megha Tex can arrange collection for large-volume orders at the client's request and expense.

  • Holders must be delivered in a safe and organized manner
  • Quantity and condition at intake will be documented
  • A receiving acknowledgment will be issued upon intake of holders
  • Clients are advised to retain a copy of their intake acknowledgment

2.2 Return of Serviced Holders

Serviced holders will be returned in protective packaging with a full service report. Return dispatch will be arranged upon completion and payment confirmation.

  • Holders are returned via safe, agreed transport method
  • Risk of loss or damage during return transport is borne by the client after dispatch
  • Any transit damage must be reported within 24 hours of receipt
  • Uncollected holders after 30 days of completion notice may attract storage charges

2.3 Loss or Damage During Service

Megha Tex takes utmost care during servicing. In the unlikely event of accidental damage caused during service, we will repair or replace the affected component at no additional cost to the client.

🤝 Customer Responsibilities

To ensure smooth and effective service, clients are expected to:

  • Provide accurate information about the type and model of spinning machinery in use
  • Inform Megha Tex of any known defects, unusual wear patterns, or operational history
  • Ensure holders are free from excessive hazardous materials or chemical contamination
  • Provide a clear point of contact for service communication
  • Respond promptly to service updates and approval requests
  • Arrange prompt collection of completed holders as notified
  • Make payment within agreed terms
Accurate information provided by the client helps us deliver better service. Megha Tex cannot be held liable for service outcomes affected by incomplete or inaccurate information.

🛡️ Warranty Limitations

4.1 Service Warranty

Megha Tex provides a 30-day service warranty on all repair and restoration work. During this period, any defect directly attributable to our workmanship will be rectified at no charge.

  • Warranty applies to the specific repair work performed
  • Warranty does not cover damage from incorrect installation or mishandling post-service
  • Warranty does not cover normal wear and tear during production
  • Warranty claims must be reported in writing within the warranty period

4.2 Exclusions

The service warranty does not cover:

  • Damage caused by incorrect fitment or improper use after return
  • Wear resulting from abnormal operating conditions
  • Failure of components not serviced or replaced by Megha Tex
  • Consequential damages including production losses
Megha Tex's liability is limited to the value of the service performed. We do not accept liability for consequential, indirect, or production-related losses.

💳 Payment Conditions

5.1 Pricing

Service pricing is quoted per batch based on the type, quantity, and condition of bobbin holders. All prices are quoted in Indian Rupees and are exclusive of applicable taxes unless stated otherwise.

  • A written quotation or verbal confirmation will be provided before service begins
  • Prices may vary based on extent of repair required
  • Emergency service requests attract a priority service surcharge
  • Quotations are valid for 7 days from date of issue

5.2 Payment Terms

  • Standard payment terms: full payment upon completion before dispatch
  • Advance payment may be required for large-volume or new client orders
  • Accepted payment methods: Bank transfer (NEFT/RTGS), UPI, or cash
  • Serviced holders will not be released until full payment is received

5.3 Late Payment

Megha Tex reserves the right to charge interest on overdue amounts and to suspend services to clients with outstanding payments.
