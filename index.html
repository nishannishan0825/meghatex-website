About Megha Tex

Trusted Bobbin Holder Service Partner for Spinning Mills

Megha Tex is a dedicated service company specializing in the inspection, cleaning, repair, restoration, and maintenance of bobbin holders used in textile spinning mills. We understand how critical precision and reliability are in the spinning process.

Based in Coimbatore — the heart of India's textile industry — we serve spinning mills across Tamil Nadu with professional technical expertise. Our team of experienced technicians ensures that every bobbin holder we service meets strict performance standards before being returned to your mill.

What We Do

Our Core Services

Comprehensive bobbin holder servicing solutions tailored to meet the specific operational demands of modern spinning mills.

01
⚙️

Bobbin Holder Servicing

Complete end-to-end servicing of bobbin holders including dismantling, inspection of all components, repair or replacement of worn parts, and precise reassembly to manufacturer specifications.

02
🧹

Bobbin Holder Cleaning

Professional deep cleaning to remove accumulated lint, dust, oil, and contaminants that affect spinning performance. We use specialized cleaning agents safe for precision components.

03
🔧

Holder Maintenance

Scheduled preventive maintenance programs to keep your bobbin holders in peak operating condition, reducing unexpected downtime and extending service life significantly.

04
♻️

Holder Restoration

Full restoration of damaged or worn-out bobbin holders back to near-original condition — a cost-effective alternative to full replacement that maintains quality output.

05
🚨

Emergency Service Support

Rapid-response emergency servicing for urgent operational failures. Our team is ready to deploy quickly to minimize production downtime and get your mill running again.
