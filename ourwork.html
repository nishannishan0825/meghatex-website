How We Service Your Bobbin Holders

Our streamlined 3-stage process ensures every bobbin holder is restored to factory-level performance before it returns to your mill.

01
🔬

Inspection & Diagnosis

Every batch begins with a thorough assessment. We examine each holder for wear patterns, bearing condition, spindle alignment, and structural damage — then document findings and build a targeted service plan.

Visual Inspection Bearing Assessment Spindle Alignment Damage Documentation Service Report
02
🧹

Professional Cleaning

Accumulated lint, lubricant residue, dust, and contaminants are fully removed using industrial-grade cleaning methods. Clean components are the foundation of reliable spinning performance.

Full Dismantling Industrial Degreasing Lint Removal Bearing Housing Clean Drying & Prep
03
🔩

Repair & Restoration

Based on inspection findings, our skilled technicians replace worn bearings, repair spindles, fix structural damage, and precisely reassemble each holder — then lubricate and verify it is ready for mill use.

Bearing Replacement Spindle Repair Spring & Clip Fix Structural Restoration Precision Lubrication
